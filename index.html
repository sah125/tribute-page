Biography

Steve Jobs, visionary and innovator, Born in 1955, co-founder of Apple, the generator. A pioneer in personal computing, he reshaped tech's scope, With Macintosh, iPhone, and iPad, he gave hope.

His passion fueled design, simplicity in each creation, Revolutionized industries, sparked a digital sensation. But beyond the gadgets, his ethos did inspire, To "think different," to dream, to aim higher.

Though his journey faced setbacks, he persevered, A legacy of innovation, his name revered. In memory of his brilliance, we honor his strides, Steve Jobs, forever in technology's annals, abides.
